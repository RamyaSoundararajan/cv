Main Project


Topic:HRM RE-HEATING FURANCE TEMPERATURE CONTROLLER USING MICROCONTROLLER


  • In our project the microcontroller is used for the measurement of the product which involves the measure of weight, length and width. If the fault measure is identified, the alarm is used for the initialization of product rejection. The fault in the size of any product can be detected and appropriate action can be carried out using this project.

  • When the product is heated up to certain amount as fixed earlier, the sensor is used to sense the heat variation and the motors of the conveyer are operated automatically for the extraction of the product for the further process of reducing the width and increasing the length of the product.
